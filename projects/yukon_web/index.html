Objective

Move all cards to the four foundation piles, building each suit from Ace to King.

Gameplay

  • Build cards downward in alternating colors (red on black, black on red).
  • Move any face-up card with all cards stacked on top of it.
  • Moved stacks do not need to be in sequence.
  • A move is valid if the bottom card follows the alternating color rule.
  • Turn over face-down cards when they are uncovered.
  • Any card or stack can be moved into an empty column.

Foundations

Build up by suit from Ace to King.

Winning

You win when all cards are placed in the foundation piles in the correct order.
