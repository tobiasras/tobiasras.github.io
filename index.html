Spotify queuer

A fullstack application, made for handling the music at the student bar at KEA.

At the bar we had a problem with people not respecting the music queue, with led to bad wibes. To solve this problem me and 2 other students decided to create a web app, letting the particapents of the bar add songs like a jukebox.

We used a react frontend, express,js as the backend. the 2 services communicated over socket.io, and REST. For handling the music features we used the Spotify API.
